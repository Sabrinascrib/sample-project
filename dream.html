My Dream

Every one has dreams... at least most of us! My Dream is it to become hokage. At first I had this dream because I was tired of being shunned and hated by the village and I wanted to gain respect from the villagers. However, as I became older and mature, I wanted to create a peaceful world. I've been seeing people getting treated unfairly and I love to help people, so becoming Hokage is a good way to gain a lot of influence and respect that would allow me to continue to lead others into an era of peace.

How To Achive A Dream:

  • Believe in yourself.
  • Focus with a positive attitude.
  • Understand why this goal is important.
  • Track your performance.
